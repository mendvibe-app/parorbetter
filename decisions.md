# Decisions

Log of gameplay-affecting decisions, added as they happen.

- Concurrent shot input: power/stance and swing timing stay live together and resolve on the impact tap (live values), so balance isn't locked before the swing the way sequential phases forced.
- Early-release (soft): lifting finger 1 before impact multiplies stability by 0.45 and caps it at 0.32 for the rest of the shot (re-grab can't fully recover); finger 2 can still finish. Playtest tunable — not final.
